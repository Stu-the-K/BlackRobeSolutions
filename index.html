Built from the Bench to the Bar

Black Robe Solutions was founded by the Hon. Stuart F. Karden (Ret.), who served as a Judge at the Immigration Court in Orlando, Florida for over 14 years. Judge Karden also served as the past chair of both the Florida Bar Immigration & Nationality certification committee and the South Florida chapter of the American Immigration Lawyers Association (AILA).

After years in private practice wrestling with complex calculations like the I-751 and N-400 filing windows, and then on the bench calculating deadlines and complex dependent dates, like initial brief and reply deadlines, Judge Karden built what has now evolved into JudgeCalc. Every feature reflects the real-world needs of legal professionals who can't afford to miss a single deadline.

Black Robe Solutions creates legal technology that actually understands how attorneys work—because Judge Karden has seen it all from both sides of the bench for nearly 50 years and counting.
